How it works

AI + rules hybrid

We combine deterministic clinical rules and coverage logic with ML ranking models so decisions are transparent and auditable — nurses and pharmacists remain in control.

  1. Ingest prescription + patient context (age, comorbidities, insurance)
  2. Run safety & interaction checks
  3. Score coverage fit and alternative options
  4. Output: suggested action + documentation bundle for dispensing or PA
